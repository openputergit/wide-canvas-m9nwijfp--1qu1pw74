About JEEV

Our Mission & Vision

JEEV was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide compassionate, high-quality home care that empowers individuals to live dignified, independent lives in the comfort of their own homes.

Our mission is to deliver personalized care plans that address the unique needs of each client, combining professional expertise with genuine empathy and respect.

Our Values

  • Professional excellence in care delivery
  • Respect for cultural diversity and personal dignity
  • Integrity and transparency in all services

Our Services

We offer comprehensive home care solutions tailored to your unique needs, delivered with compassion and professionalism.

Elderly Care

Comprehensive companion care, personal assistance, and medication management for seniors who wish to maintain their independence while receiving the support they need.

  • Personal hygiene assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Companionship and emotional support

Post-Surgery Care

Professional support during recovery periods after hospital discharge, ensuring proper healing and reducing readmission risks.

  • Wound care assistance
  • Mobility support
  • Medication administration

Disability Care

Specialized assistance for individuals with disabilities, focusing on enhancing independence and quality of life.

  • Personal care and grooming
  • Mobility and transfer assistance
  • Community access support

Home Nursing

Professional nursing services delivered in the comfort of your home by qualified healthcare professionals.

  • IV therapy and injections
  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Complex medication management

Palliative Care

Compassionate care focused on providing relief from the symptoms and stress of serious illness, improving quality of life.

  • Pain management
  • Emotional and spiritual support
  • Family guidance and support

Specialized Care

Tailored care for specific conditions such as dementia, Parkinson's, and other specialized needs.

  • Dementia and Alzheimer's care
  • Parkinson's disease support
  • Stroke recovery assistance
